How to submit an article or a study

Jack Mehof


Not seeing what you are looking for, here is the instructions for how to submit an article or a study.

First, you will want to write your paper in the proper format, here is how to do it:

Write your article in HTML5. It should be formatted like:

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang="en">
<head>
<meta charset="UTF-8">
<title>Title of your article | PISNT</title>
</head>
<body>
<h1>Title of your article/study</h1>
<h2>Author name or psudonym</h2>
<br>
<h3>Subheading (if applicable)</h3>
<p>Article; you can use more paragrah tags, breaks, and subheadings as needed.</p>
</body>
</html>

Send your article request to perangoriginal@gmail.com as a .html file. If you have any requests of formatting additions that deviate from the base formatting rules or questions, send them to the same email. Your article will be reviewed and you will get an acception or rejection letter ASAP. If you have no idea what any of this is, check out an HTML tutorial.
